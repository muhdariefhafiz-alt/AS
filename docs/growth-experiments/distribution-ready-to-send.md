# Ready-to-send distribution copy: agent-statistics study

Asset: https://fair-comparisons.com/insights/property-agent-statistics-singapore

Real numbers (from the study; keep exact):
- 38,110 agents on the register; only ~12,960 (about 1 in 3) appear in the CEA transaction record. So **66% have no transaction on file**.
- Only ~10,981 (under 30%) transacted in 2024-2025.
- **63% of all agent activity is rentals**, not home sales.
- **The top 20% of active agents handle about 70% of all home sales** (top 10% = 52%).
- Among agents who have ever recorded a sale (~12,400), the **median did 8 sales in 8.5 years - about one a year**.
