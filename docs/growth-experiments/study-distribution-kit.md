# Distribution kit: "Singapore Property Agent Statistics" study

Goal: earn links and citations for the data study at
`/insights/property-agent-statistics-singapore`, because a young domain
moves from page 2 to page 1 on **authority (links)**, not more pages. A
data study is the single most linkable asset we have.

**The asset:** https://fair-comparisons.com/insights/property-agent-statistics-singapore

**The hook stats (all real, from the page; update if the figures move):**
- 66% of registered agents have no transaction on file (only ~12,960 of 38,110 appear in the CEA record).
- Just under 30% transacted at all in 2024-2025.
- 63% of all agent activity is rentals, not sales.
- The top 20% of active agents handle 70% of all home sales (top 10% = 52%).
- The median agent who sells closes about one home a year.

**Guardrails (do not break these):**
- Every number must trace to the study. No invented stats ("X agents joined").
- Do not name the founder anywhere. Attribute to FairComparisons.
- Value-first. Lead with the data finding, not the product. Communities remove promo.
- Reflect the **current** model: free for sellers, no success fee, no cut of any sale; agents claim their profile free, optional subscription tools only. (The old success-fee framing in exp-04/exp-05 is out of date - ignore it.)
- Always disclose it is your project when posting in communities.
