# Plan: get AI engines citing FairComparisons for SG property-agent searches

Budget cap: S$500, one-off. Timeframe: ~3 weeks to launch, then a monthly measurement loop.
Guiding evidence: AI engines cite brand mentions, original data, listicles, Reddit and Wikipedia,
not paid wire syndication (0.04% of AI citations). So the money is small and disciplined; the
effort goes into earned, owned and community signals. Integrity rule holds throughout: nothing
that looks like a bought ranking, no fabricated data, founder stays unnamed.

Owner key: [C] = Claude can produce/do, [L] = Lex must do (spend, real-email sends, accounts, go/no-go).

## Phase 1 — Free, highest-ROI foundations (week 1, S$0)

These are the levers the research ranked above any paid option.

1. [C] Build 2 to 3 citable data pages from the CEA dataset, formatted for AI extraction:
   clear question-style H2s, a ranked table, dated stat callouts, sources named. Examples:
   "Median property agent commission by town, 2025" and "How to compare a Singapore property
   agent's track record". Comparative tables and original stats are the two highest-citing formats.
2. [C] Add or confirm structured data on key pages: Organization, ItemList, FAQ schema; ensure
   GPTBot and PerplexityBot are not blocked in robots.txt; confirm freshness dates render.
3. [C] Draft one authentic Reddit post for r/singaporefi plus 3 ready replies for recurring
   "how do I pick an agent" threads. Brand-first and pseudonymous to respect anonymity; always
   disclose the affiliation ("I work on FairComparisons"); lead with the free tool, not a link dump.
4. [L] Post and engage on Reddit over the week. Do not blitz; follow each sub's self-promo rules.

Exit gate: data pages live and indexable, schema in place, Reddit presence started.

## Phase 2 — The S$500 paid spend (week 1 to 2)

Decision gate first, then spend. Two mutually exclusive paths.

**Step 1 [L]:** Request a quote from Media OutReach Newswire (media-outreach.com/en-sg).
It is the only wire with guaranteed verbatim postings on Mediacorp/CNA and SPH Media, the most
credible paid placement available locally.

- If the quote is at or under ~S$450 → **Path A**.
- If it is over budget → **Path B**.

**Path A (preferred): Media OutReach single release**
- [L] Buy one release, distribute the Phase 0 release. Spend ~S$400 to S$450. Reserve the rest.

**Path B (fallback): budget wire + listicles**
- [L] EIN Presswire single release, US$149 (~S$200). Legitimate Google News and AP indexing,
  no link-scheme risk. Treat as visibility and a permanent dated record, not SEO equity.
- [C] Draft pitch + [L] pay for inclusion in 1 to 2 genuinely curated "best property comparison
  tools / how to choose an agent in Singapore" listicles, ~S$150 to S$250 total. Skip link-farm
  directories. Listicles are roughly a third of AI citations, so this is the better AI lever.

Hard avoid (ineffective and brand-toxic): "guaranteed Yahoo/AP dofollow" packages, Fiverr
"7 dofollow links" sellers, King Newswire / IMCWire and similar. Dofollow PR links are a Google
link-scheme risk and AI ignores the duplicates.

Exit gate: one credible paid distribution live, within S$500, with receipts logged below.

## Phase 4 — Measure (ongoing, monthly)

1. [C] Provide a 10-query checklist (e.g. "best property agent comparison Singapore",
   "how to check a Singapore property agent track record") to run monthly across ChatGPT,
   Perplexity and Google AI Overviews; log whether FairComparisons is cited.
2. [L] Track brand search volume ("fair comparisons singapore") as the leading indicator,
   since branded search is a top predictor of AI visibility.
3. Review after 4 weeks: double down on whatever produced a citation, drop what did not.
