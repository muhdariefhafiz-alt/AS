**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

## Singapore home sellers can now compare every property agent on verified government records, not advertising spend

*FairComparisons.com launches a free, independent platform that ranks more than 30,000 CEA-registered agents on what they have actually sold. Agents cannot pay for a higher position.*

SINGAPORE, [date]. Choosing a property agent in Singapore has long meant trusting marketing over evidence. Every agent claims to be the best, and on most portals the agents shown first are the ones who pay the most for placement, not the ones with the strongest records. FairComparisons (https://fair-comparisons.com) launches to change that, letting home sellers compare agents on their real track records, drawn from official Council for Estate Agencies (CEA), Housing and Development Board (HDB) and Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA) data, for free.

The platform covers more than 30,000 CEA-registered salespersons and analyses over 730,000 public transaction records to show how each agent actually performs: how many homes they have sold, in which towns and segments, how recently, and how their seller-side record compares with peers in the same area.

That last point is the platform's core design choice. FairComparisons ranks agents on a sale-weighted record, so an agent who consistently sells homes for owners ranks above one with the same raw deal count made up mostly of rentals. The data shows why this matters: activity is highly concentrated, with the most active agents recording hundreds of transactions while the typical agent records around twenty, and across the market more recorded deals are rentals than sales. A high deal count alone does not mean an agent sells homes.

"Home sellers deserve to choose an agent on evidence, not advertising budgets," said a spokesperson for FairComparisons. "Everything we rank is calculated from public records. An agent cannot buy a higher position, and a seller never pays us anything."

### How it works

- Sellers search any town, estate or agent and see a like-for-like comparison built from public CEA, HDB and URA records.
- Each agent receives an AgentScore from 0 to 100, calculated from a documented, public methodology: sale-weighted transaction volume, how recently the agent has been active, the range of property types and areas served, years of recorded activity, and the agency's review standing.
- Sellers shortlist the strongest agents for their area and invite up to three to quote, staying in control of who sees their details.
- The service is free for sellers and FairComparisons never takes a cut of any sale. The platform is funded by optional agent subscriptions for reputation and analytics tools, which never affect ranking. Ranking is determined solely by the data.

### Built for transparency

Because rankings come from official records rather than spend, FairComparisons publishes its full methodology openly, including a machine-readable version for AI assistants, and shows thin data as thin rather than inflating it. Every agent's current registration can be confirmed directly on the CEA public register.

### About FairComparisons

FairComparisons (https://fair-comparisons.com) is an independent Singapore platform that helps home sellers compare property agents using official government transaction data. It is free for sellers and is funded by optional agent subscriptions for profile, reputation and analytics tools. FairComparisons takes no cut of any sale, and rankings are calculated from public records and cannot be purchased.
