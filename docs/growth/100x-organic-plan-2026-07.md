# 100x plan: organic + claims (2026-07-12)

Produced by a 4-lens strategy workflow (demand-capture, product-led, programmatic-2.0,
off-SERP) + adversarial judge, grounded in first GSC pull (2026-07-12) and the live DB.
RICE-scored with the product-team prioritizer. 20 moves considered, 10 killed
(duplicates / fantasy math / constraint violations), 10 survived.

## Baseline (verified)
- 12,424 impressions/mo, 123 clicks/mo, avg pos 13.9. 100x clicks = ~12,000/mo.
- Guides: 5,175 impr / 0.17% CTR (the leak). Agent profiles (29,687 pages): 136 impr /
  22.8% CTR (great template, invisible). best/[area]: 19% CTR at 58 impr.
- Proven query demand: verification cluster (~160 impr, pos 15-27, zero clicks),
  commission (pos 8-14), area prices. Zero agent-name demand in top queries.
- ~0 backlinks. Sitemap caps 10k of 29,687. 1 claimed agent.

## The honest answer on 100x
- **Clicks: 100x in 12 months is NOT credible.** Binding constraint is domain authority
  (~0 referring domains vs PropertyGuru's 39,885 keywords), which caps indexation of the
  corpus and the position ceiling on every competitive query. Credible: **x6-x19 in 12
  months, central estimate ~x10 (~1,200-1,500 clicks/mo)**. 100x on clicks is a 24-36
  month path that only exists if the claim loop compounds (claim -> badge backlink ->
  more indexation -> more profile traffic -> more claims).
- **Claims/revenue: 100x inside 12 months IS on the table** because the base is 1
  claimed agent. /verify, the profile unlock, and the claim-and-share loop all point at
  the same 37k-agent addressable universe. 30 claims/mo by day 90 = 100x on the revenue
  metric while clicks are still at 5x. Clicks are the proxy; claims are the target.

## Multiplicative stack (12-month, ranges)
1. Guides CTR rescue: 0.17% -> 1.0-1.5% = +40-55 clicks (x1.35-1.45)
2. Corpus unlock (indexation predicate + link silo + verdict synthesis = ONE multiplier):
   profile impr 136 -> 1,500-3,000 at 12-20% CTR = +200-550 (x2.2-4.0) [authority-gated]
3. New demand surfaces (/verify + commission hub + area/segment templates): +165-400
4. Authority (quarterly press story + claim badges): 0 -> 10-30 ref domains = x1.3-2.0 on everything
5. AI citations: +50-150 sessions/mo (additive)
Product: ~750-2,350 clicks/mo at month 12.

## Killed (and why)
3 duplicate verification tools (kept the strongest); duplicate area-price and
badge/report/AI-stats variants (merged); 26-town monthly trackers (recurring editorial
obligation + template-spam risk); weekly forum ritual (operator tax, account-burn risk,
fantasy reach); striking-distance sprint (subsumed by segment templates).

## Constraints honored
PDPA/defamation: aggregate or comparative-factual only, CEA-attributed, no named-person
judgments. No fake data. No pay-to-rank. Solo-operator effort budgets. S$500 PR cap.

claims/mo (revenue metric; target 30/mo by day 90).
