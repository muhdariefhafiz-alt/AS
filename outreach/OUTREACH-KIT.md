## Sending strategy

1. Start with the **top 5** (most transactions = most to gain from a claimed profile)
2. Send **max 5 per day** to avoid being flagged as spam from a new domain
3. Send Mon-Thu between 9-11am SGT (agents check email before viewings)
4. Follow up after 3 days if no response (different subject line: "Quick question about your [AREA] profile")
5. Track responses in this file - mark who opened, replied, claimed
