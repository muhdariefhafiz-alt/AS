# SG media + partnership outreach list

GetAgent's distribution was MoneySavingExpert + Which? (trust partners) + Channel 4
media-for-equity + programmatic SEO. SG has no Channel 4 equivalent, so the play
is **trust-partner content + finance communities + property editorial**, anchored
by your one genuinely ownable angle:

> **The pitch, in one line:** "Singapore's only independent property-agent
> comparison ranked on actual CEA transaction data — not advertising. We can
> tell your readers, with real numbers, which agents actually sell fastest and
> closest to asking in their town. Free for sellers."

That data angle (CEA transaction-record rankings) is editorial gold — it's a
data story no one else in SG can write. Lead with offering **data, not a banner**.

---

These have the high-intent, trust-rich homeowner audience. Partnership > ad.

## Tier 4 — Owned + lifecycle (your highest-ROI "channel")

Not media, but the cheapest demand you have. The board flagged this as the
realistic GetAgent-media-for-equity substitute.

- **MOP-lifecycle email list** — the MOP tracker builds a list of owners 1–12
  months from selling eligibility. This is *owned, high-intent, predictable*
  demand. Prioritise filling it (the MOP tracker is already live).
- **AVM "track my value" list** — same logic, 6–18 months earlier in the journey.
- **CEA agent outreach** — WhatsApp/email the high-volume agents (the SPAD-style
  list); "your CEA record is live, claim your free profile, get seller leads."

## Outreach sequence (suggested order)

1. **Week 1:** Stacked Homes + EdgeProp (data-story pitches — fastest credibility,
   they want proprietary data). One exclusive dataset each.
2. **Week 1–2:** Seedly + MoneySmart (partnership conversations — longer cycle).
3. **Week 2:** r/singaporefi authentic launch post + CNA/TODAY commentary pitch.
4. **Ongoing:** ST/BT for the big MOP-wave timing feature; lifecycle email list
   compounding underneath it all.

## What to send them

A one-page "data brief": 3–5 genuinely interesting findings from your 730k
transactions (e.g. "agents in the top decile sell X% closer to asking," "median
days-to-sell varies Nx across towns"), the methodology (CEA public records), and
the offer ("we'll build a custom cut for your readers / co-author"). **Real
numbers only — no invented stats** (your own rule). Pull the findings from the
live data before pitching.

## Anonymity note

Per your constraint, you stay unnamed publicly — pitch as "the platform" /
"FairComparisons," not under your own name. All of the above works brand-first.
